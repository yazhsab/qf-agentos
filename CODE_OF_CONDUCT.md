# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
QF-AgentOS community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
public or private harassment, publishing others' private information without
permission, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate
in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project maintainers at `conduct@qf-agentos.org`. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated
to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
